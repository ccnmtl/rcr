Thomas H. Murray, PhD, is President of The Hastings Center. His research interests include ethical issues involving parents and children, genetics, and enhancement. His publications include The Worth of a Child and he is an editor of the Encyclopedia of Ethical, Legal and Policy Issues in Biotechnology.
