»Learning Objectives«

  1. Articulate why it is important to identify conflicts of interest.
  2. Define conflict of interest.
  3. Define and describe specific types and levels of conflicts of interest.
  4. Articulate why it is imperative to manage conflicts of interest in clinical research.
  5. Define and identify issues in institutional conflict of interest.
  6. Define and identify issues in IRB members conflicts of interest.
  7. Understand the specific guidelines in government policies on conflicts of interest.
  8. Articulate the problematic areas of governmental policies on conflicts of interest.
  9. View examples of conflict of interest policies from institutions and professional organizations.
  10. Discuss strategies for managing conflicts of interest.
