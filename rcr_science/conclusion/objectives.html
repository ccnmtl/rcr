»Learning Objectives«

  1. Recognize the increased tendency toward collaborative research in many fields.
  2. Identify the pitfalls that may occur in collaborative research.
  3. Gain familiarity with ways to enhance good collaborative research.
  4. Understand the institutional processes involved in collaborative research.
  5. Examine some of the ethical considerations in collaborative research.
  6. Appreciate the resources available to deal with collaborative research.
