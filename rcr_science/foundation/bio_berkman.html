Dr. Alan Berkman
Department of Epidemiology
Mailman School of Public Health

BACKGROUND
Dr. Berkman is a graduate of Columbia P&S and trained in internal medicine at Presbyterian Hospital; he has also done a post-doctoral fellowship in HIV prevention research at the HIV Center for Clinical and Behavioral Studies at the New York State Psychiatric Institute and Columbia University. He has extensive experience in the clinical management and clinical research of HIV disease both inside the US and in developing countries. He is a member of the Executive Committee of CAPRISA, a research consortium that links Columbia with four South African universities and research institutes. He served on the NYC Department of Health IRB for five years and developed a particular interest in the ethical conduct of research that involves collaboration between scientists from the US and colleagues from developing countries.
