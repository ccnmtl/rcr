3. Trust is one of the central issues in science. What might Julio have done to feel better about working with Samantha if he didn't think highly of her?

If Julio didn't feel comfortable with Samantha performing the analytic experiments, he probably should have said something to Dr. Chan about his concerns. Even though there was some pressure to move the synthesis experiments along, he could have insisted on doing the analytic experiments himself or watched as Samantha did them to ensure that they were done correctly.
