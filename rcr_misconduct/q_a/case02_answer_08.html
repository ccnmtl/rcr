8. What are some of the consequences of Sandra's actions?

An inquiry would probably be initiated at the university, and this would lead to an investigation, which might find that Sandra committed misconduct. She probably would be asked to leave her graduate program. New training protocols might be instituted at the university to train people about the proper conduct of surveys in the field. Newspaper articles and television reports might make public the findings of misconduct. As a result of the publicity, Dr. Lockheart might think she is being shunned by her colleagues. The government might also consider holding back funding for the rest of the five-year project, pending an investigation.
