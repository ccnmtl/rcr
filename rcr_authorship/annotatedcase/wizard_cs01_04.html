Ethics Wizard

What to do if a problem arises with authorship: Confronting an authority figure who has power over one’s future can be challenging, and confronting a colleague about who should be listed on a paper may be difficult, too. Although "Choose one’s battles wisely" is a cliché, it can be appropriate in determining the cost and benefit of creating an issue of authorship and credit for publication. A junior person’s not getting appropriate authorship credit could be devastating to his or her career. Before addressing the issue, it is important that the junior person understand the customs and approaches to authorship within the laboratory.
