Ethics Wizard

Discuss in advance: It can be difficult to assert needs in a new relationship, such as with a new mentor, laboratory head, or collaborator, because of fear that an early conflict might sour the tie before the association matures. So new graduate students, technicians, and postdoctoral fellows, and even senior investigators, might avoid discussing authorship and publication credit when they first come to a laboratory. Likewise, collaborators starting a new project might delay addressing authorship. But considering the issue sooner -- even by having a written document, which may be subject to change -- is better than being disappointed later.
