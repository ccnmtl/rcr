Peer review and controversial research: One of the criticisms of the peer-review system is that individuals with novel ideas will not get their voices heard either in new grant applications or in articles submitted for review. People subscribing to the current paradigm in a discipline may think that a new approach is outrageous and may not approve such a challenging article or grant application under review. How can the publications, the grant applications, and the peer-review process encourage new ideas?
