»Learning Objectives«

  1. Recognize the importance of the responsible authorship.
  2. Identify the role of an author in writing an academic paper in the sciences and the arts.
  3. Characterize the role of a peer reviewer in assessing a paper or grant application.
  4. Examine the major ethical issues surrounding publication and peer review.
  5. Understand some of the ways to deal with controversies or conflicts that might arise in authorship or peer review.
  6. Gain familiarity with resources about publication and peer review.
  7. Reflect on the way one handles the role of author, editor, or peer reviewer.
