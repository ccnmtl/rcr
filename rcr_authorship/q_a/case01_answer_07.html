7. What kind of problems may arise if the same data is used in multiple papers in the research literature?

Repetitive data is most problematic in clinical research. If the same data is used in multiple papers, without acknowledgment by the author, the literature becomes biased because it gives too much weight to one set of data. If the finding is about the beneficial effects of a drug, for example, an analysis of the literature would give the drug more positive weight than it probably should have.
