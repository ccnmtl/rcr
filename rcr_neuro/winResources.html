1: Annotated References on Data Management

Macrina F. Scientific Integrity: An Introductory Text with Cases, Second Edition, 2000 ASM Press, Chap. 9, Ownership and Data and Intellectual Property; Chap. 11, Scientific Recordkeeping. Francis Macrina gives a detailed overview of data-management debates and practice.

Council on Governmental Relations, 1996. Policy Considerations: Access to and Retention of Research Data. http://www.cogr.edu/docs/RetentionData.pdf This report summarizes the controversies surrounding data definition, data storage, and data sharing and retention.

Steneck N. Introduction to the Responsible Conduct of Research, ORI, Chap. 6: Data Management Practices. http://ori.dhhs.gov/multimedia/acrobat/rcrintro.pdf Nicholas Steneck provides a concise presentation on most of the issues in data management and offers lists that highlight the main points to consider in data management.
