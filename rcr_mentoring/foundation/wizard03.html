Ethics Wizard

You should be aware not only of policies and procedures dealing with mentoring, or with resolution of problems, but also of other policies and procedures that might apply to these situations - for example, if sexual harassment is involved, or in an allegation of research misconduct. Find out what written policies exist at your university.

Relevant Columbia University policies include:

(1) Statement on Professional Ethics and Faculty Obligations and Guidelines for Review of Professional Misconduct

(2) Guidelines for Review of Misconduct in Science For The Columbia University's Health Science Campus

(3) Protection Against Sexual Harassment
