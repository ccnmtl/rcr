»Presidential Award«

About 10 individuals and 8-10 institutions have received awards every year since 1996. Typical qualities for which recipients have been rewarded are:

  • recruiting in graduate programs
  • producing doctoral and master's-level students
  • offering the opportunity to experience groundbreaking laboratory research
  • earning doctorates and master's degrees
  • securing support for students
  • placing students in research settings
  • completing degrees in a timely manner
  • encouraging students to take advantage of opportunities such as internships, publications, and conference activities
  • successfully securing funds and fellowships for graduate students
  • publications co-authored by students
  • involving students in significant research and supporting their efforts

The program is now administered through the National Science Foundation. More information is available at: http://www.ehr.nsf.gov/EHR/HRD/paesmem.asp.
