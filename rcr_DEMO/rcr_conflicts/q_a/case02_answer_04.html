4. Is there an upper limit to the amount of compensation that Dr. Cardoza can receive from Mayan Adventures before he must disclose this to university officials? Does he also have to disclose this extra income to the National Science Foundation?

The upper limit of compensation that Dr. Cardoza must disclose to his university depends on the university’s policy. Dr. Cardoza must report to the National Science Foundation extra income if it exceeds the $10,000 limit over a 12-month period. This is the current limit for the United States Public Health Service, and is subject to change.
